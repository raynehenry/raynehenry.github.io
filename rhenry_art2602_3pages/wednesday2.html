What I did

Last Wednesday

A Short Story by Rayne Henry

Once upon a time, on Wednesday, January 12th, 2022, things happened.
You may be asking yourself, "What things happened?". Well, the answer is simple: interesting things.

Now, I know what you may be asking yourself, "Why the hell won't they get to the point?". I am happy to inform you that the answer to that is... just kidding! Calm down, jeez.

Once upon a time, on the date previously mentioned, I witnessed a bee fly into the park. This bee, named Constantine III, landed on a small flower and began to pollinate it.
You see, this was Constantine's first time pollinating a flower; she was nervous.

Beginning to sweat, Constantine took a deep breath and was about to continue, when she was suddenly crushed to death by a large human man named Jeff.

Un{bee}known to Jeff, this accidental manslaughter would spark a political controversy and future coup in Constantine's old colony.

Her queen, an esteemed leader and member of the Bee7, would die in this coup.

From there, WWBee would break out and have dire consequences, for bee, and man, kind.

To find out exactly what, come back next Wednesday for the next installment of "What I did Last Wednesday".
